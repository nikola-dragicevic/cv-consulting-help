# JobbNu Brand Foundation (v1)

## Core Mission
Help as many people as possible into work, with a concrete long-term target of **10,000 people**.

This mission should appear in internal docs, investor material, and selected external touchpoints.

## 3 Company Principles

### 1) Every Person Matters
**Meaning:** No user is treated as a number. If one person asks for help, we do the work with full seriousness.

**Behavior standard:**
- We answer clearly, quickly, and respectfully.
- We turn confusion into concrete next steps.
- We optimize for real progress toward interviews and offers.

**Voice example:**
- "One profile at a time. One breakthrough at a time."

### 2) Real Help, Real Results
**Meaning:** We do not sell vague inspiration. We deliver tools and guidance that improve job outcomes.

**Behavior standard:**
- Recommendations must be specific and actionable.
- Product features must reduce time to application and improve fit quality.
- We measure what matters: interviews, response rate, and hires.

**Voice example:**
- "From effort to outcome."

### 3) Fair Access to Opportunity
**Meaning:** Professional job support should be affordable and available, not a luxury.

**Behavior standard:**
- Keep pricing transparent and reasonable.
- Keep onboarding simple and fast.
- Remove friction for users who need immediate support.

**Voice example:**
- "High-quality support at a fair price."

## Symbolism Strategy

Your belief: **symbols are portals that radiate power**.

This can be translated into a brand system where the symbol communicates:
- **Portal:** an opening from uncertainty to opportunity.
- **Radiance:** active support flowing outward.
- **Human center:** each person is the core of the process.
- **Collective impact:** many people helped, scaling toward 10,000.

### Shape semantics
- Outer ring: trust, protection, consistency.
- Inner gate/portal form: transition into a new career phase.
- Upward central line: direction, growth, forward movement.
- Three rays: effort, guidance, and momentum radiating outward.
- Small outer points: many individuals reached over time.

## Suggested Brand Message Stack
- **Mission line:** "We help people move into work. Goal: 10,000 lives changed."
- **Promise line:** "Practical support. Fair pricing. Real progress."
- **Emotional line:** "When you feel stuck, there is still a way forward."

## Tagline Options
- "Open the way to your next job."
- "From uncertainty to opportunity."
- "Real help. Real jobs."

## Quick Use Rules
- Use full-color symbol on white or very light background.
- Use mono symbol for invoices, legal docs, and embossing.
- Keep clear space around symbol equal to the symbol's center circle radius.
- Do not stretch, rotate, or add effects.
